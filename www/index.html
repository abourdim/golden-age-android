Faith & Knowledge

The Quran commanded Muslims to read, think, and discover.

Over 1,000 years ago, while much of the world had forgotten the great works of ancient civilizations, Muslims built a culture of knowledge that changed everything.

It started with one word revealed to the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ : Iqra — Read.

From that moment, Muslims understood that seeking knowledge was not separate from faith — it was faith. Every discovery was an act of worship.

The Islamic Golden Age began not with money or power, but with Bismillah.

اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ
"Read! In the name of your Lord who created."
Surah Al-Alaq 96:1
هَلْ يَسْتَوِي الَّذِينَ يَعْلَمُونَ وَالَّذِينَ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
"Are those who know equal to those who do not know?"
Surah Az-Zumar 39:9

📖 About This App

This interactive web app teaches children aged 7–12 about the Islamic Golden Age (roughly 750–1258 CE) — a period when Muslim scholars led the world in science, medicine, mathematics, engineering, and the arts. The app integrates faith and education in an age-appropriate, engaging way.

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand the Quranic command to seek knowledge
  • Learn about 12+ major Muslim scholars and their contributions
  • Explore 8 scientific fields advanced during the Golden Age
  • Discover how everyday items (coffee, cameras, hospitals) have Islamic origins
  • Practice Arabic pronunciation and Quranic recitation
  • Build pride in Islamic heritage and motivation for learning

🕹️ Features & Activities

  • 35+ sections covering history, science, geography, language, and art
  • 13 interactive science demos (algebra, optics, surgery tools, etc.)
  • 7 games: matching, memory, word scramble, true/false, timeline ordering, pattern builder, quiz
  • Text-to-speech for all content including Arabic pronunciation
  • Speech recognition for pronunciation practice and quiz answers
  • Achievement badges and personalized certificate

🔒 Privacy & Safety

  • No data is sent to any server — everything runs in the browser
  • Speech recognition processes audio locally via the Web Speech API
  • Progress is saved to localStorage (can be reset at any time)
  • No ads, tracking, or third-party scripts
  • No illustrations of human faces (emoji silhouettes used throughout)

💡 Tips for Parents & Teachers

  • Sit together for the first exploration — children love showing what they discover
  • Use the narration mode (🔊 button) for younger children who are still learning to read
  • The Story Mode section is perfect for bedtime reading
  • Encourage children to try the pronunciation challenge together
  • The certificate at the end makes a great printable reward
  • Works best on Chrome or Safari for speech features
